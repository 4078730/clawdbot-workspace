The Problem We Solve

SCM operations — warehousing, packaging, assembly — remain heavily dependent on manual labor due to four fundamental challenges.

🗣️

Ambiguous Instructions

Interpreting "pack it tightly" requires reasoning about implicit rules — heavy items at bottom, fragile on top.

🤖

SIer Dependency

Every new SKU requires expert reprogramming. Natural language instruction eliminates this bottleneck.

📦

120K SKU Variety

Bags, boxes, foam-wrapped parts, loose screws — each requires different grasp strategies impossible to pre-program.

⚡

Dynamic Recovery

Grasp failures, drops, unexpected states — robots must autonomously recover without human intervention.

Challenges Toward Robo Sync AI Suite

Integrating VLA into RoboSync as a commercial platform — technical and platform-level challenges.

T Technical Challenges

T1: Coordinate ↔ Joint Control Bridge

RoboSync uses task-space open-loop; VLA uses joint-space closed-loop. Seamless switching needed.

T2: VLA Safety Guardrails

Speed/force limits, anomaly detection, emergency stop integration with RoboSync safety PLC.

T3: Generalization at Scale

120K SKUs with diverse shapes and packaging. End-effector strategy selection.

T4: Agentic Multi-Skill Control

Dynamic skill switching (pick → pack → seal → place). Autonomous failure recovery.
